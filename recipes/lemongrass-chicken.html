Lemongrass Chicken

Description

description of the lemongrass chicken

Ingredients

Chicken and Marinade

  • 1.5-2 lb. chicken, boneless and skinless
  • 2 tbsp. minced lemongrass
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp. fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. honey (or sugar)
  • 2 tbsp. lime juice
  • 5 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp. black pepper

Fish Sauce

  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp. white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp. lime juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 12 oz coconut soda/coco rico (or water)

To Serve

  • cooked rice or vermicelli noodles
  • cilantro, handful
  • lettuce, finely chopped
  • cucumbers, sliced
  • fish sauce

Steps

  1. Combine chicken and marinade ingredients and set aside for at least 3 hours, or overnight for best results.
  2. Heat 1/2 tbsp. oil in skillet over medium. Once hot, add chicken and cook until chicken is cooked through, about 10 minutes total, making sure the chicken doesn't burn.
  3. Remove chicken and set aside. Combine fish sauce ingredients and mix well to dissolve sugar. Adjust spiciness, lime, and sweetness to taste.
  4. Serve with cooked white rice or vermicelli noodles, lettuce, cilantro, cucumbers, and fish sauce. Enjoy!
