Lemongrass Chicken

Description

description of the lemongrass chicken

Ingredients

Chicken and Marinade

  • 1.5-2 lb. chicken, boneless and skinless
  • 2 tbsp. minced lemongrass
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp. fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. honey (or sugar)
  • 2 tbsp. lime juice
  • 5 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp. black pepper

Fish Sauce

  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp. white vinegar
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red chili, finely chopped
  • 12 oz coconut soda/coco rico (or water)
